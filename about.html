About Us

Weekly Practices

Practices on the South Lawn occur every week, typically on Wednesday Afternoons. Multiple lines are rigged ranging from beginner to advanced. No experience is necessary. Bring an insturment, a speaker, and a good attitude.

Trips Near and Far

The club hosts trips in the Charlottesville areas where members can be introduced to dizzingly high but unbeliveably fun sport of highling, slacklining high up off the ground with a harness, as well as, trips in areas nearby in West Virginia to slackline in beautiful places like Senece Rocks. See Trips for more information.

Circus Clinics

With a former acrobat and member of the circus as our president, many of the practices include personalized instruction on how to do backflips, juggle, juggle and slackline together, and other various acrobatic stunts. Come to practices to find out more.

Ground Lines, Midlines, and Highlines

Afraid of heights? No problem. Come to our weekly practice for fun ground lines as little as a few inches off the ground. For those who are looking more of a thrill stay on the lookout in the GroupMe for opportunities to suspend yourself high up off the ground and discover the wildly fun sport of highlining and midlining
